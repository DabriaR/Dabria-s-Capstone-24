Overview

Background

Our initial research topic was about the lack of black and queer representation in media. This leads me to discover that due to seeing less diversity, people are more likely to perpetuated set stereotypical ideas of how they believe black and queer people act. This led us to the idea of creating an app that allows people to access movies that have already been reviewed on how diverse they are or not and rate it on a scale of 1-5.

Danger of No Media Representations

The dangers of no media representation are significant. It can lead to marginalized communities feeling ignored, misunderstood, or misrepresented which perpetuates stereotypes and biases. Lack of representation can also limit diverse persepectives and contribute to a narrow worldview. Aditionally, it can affect social progress by silencing voices and preventing important issues from being adressed.

Blurb About Us

Our names are Dabria Reed, Ai Nghi Diep and Michelle Vong. We are all seniors in the Computer Science Academy at Oakland Tech. We choose to make this website because as minorities and teenagers, we know the effects of seing very little no people who have similiar attributes to us. We all were also able to compare what it was like to to watch a film with accurate representation rather than an all white, straight cast.
